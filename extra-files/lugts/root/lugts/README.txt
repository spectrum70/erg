Board used: Banana Pi BPI-M2 ZERO
CPU: Allwinner H2+ (eol) or H3 (depends from board rev)
512MB DDR 3 SDRAM
40 Pin Raspberry Pi-compatible GPIO, including UART, SPI, I2C, etc.

40 PIN GPIO connections used

    1 - Vcc 3V3
    2 - Vcc 5V     ===> Display i2c, VCC
    3 - TWI0-SDA   ===> Display i2c, SDA
    4 - Vcc 5V
    5 - TWI0-SCL   ===> Display i2c, SCL
    6 - GND        ===> Display i2c, GnD
